* WPS Cleanup
- wps
Open PS (Powershell) and check
#+begin_src sh
Get-Process -Name wps
#+end_src
[[./images/win-wps-process.png]]
\\
To stop WPS
#+begin_src sh
Stop-Process -Name wps -Force
#+end_src
[[./images/win-wps-stop.png]]

- wpscloudsvr
Open PS (Powershell) and check
#+begin_src sh
Get-Process -Name wpscloudsvr
Stop-Process -Name wpscloudsvr -Force
#+end_src
